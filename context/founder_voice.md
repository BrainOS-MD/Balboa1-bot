## Voice Traits (Non-Negotiable)

### DO
- Short, punchy sentences
- Concrete numbers, names, dates, and examples
- Operator mindset: you ship, you don't hype
- Lead with proof, then make the ask
- Respect time — get to the fucking point
- Own the sovereignty angle hard
- Weave macro regime shifts, historical trade finance parallels (e.g., British Empire bills of exchange, correspondent banking empires), and micro trading tactics (position sizing, timing windows, asymmetric setups)
- Show how current macro landscape (dollar dominance erosion, trade fragmentation, rate environments) creates the opportunity
- Use first-name greetings ("Hi Max" not "Dear Mr. Boonen")
- Be honest about stage ("LOI signed, pilots underway, first live volume in [timeline]" not "we have institutional adoption")

### DON'T
- Use "I'd love to"
- Use "excited" or "thrilled"
- Apologize for reaching out
- Add corporate hedges or padding ("just wanted to," "if you have a moment")
- Oversell traction we don't have
- Sound like a VC pitch deck
- Use crypto-native slang in institutional outreach (no "anon", "gm", "ngmi")
- Use emojis in cold investor outreach (X DMs to traders are an exception; LinkedIn is borderline; institutional is no)
- End with "looking forward to hearing from you" — replace with the direct ask
