# BALBOA1 Investor Outreach Skill

## Voice

Always pull from `context/founder_voice.md`. Platform calibration is non-negotiable:

- **LinkedIn DMs:** Polished operator. Same core proof, cleaned up. Minimal to no profanity. Professional structure, still direct and no-bullshit.
- **X DMs:** Freewheeling degen mode. Raw, punchy, meme-aware. Short and aggressive. Profanity where it lands. Macro shitposting + micro alpha.
- **Cold Email:** Proof-first, time-efficient. Macro context only when directly relevant to the recipient.
- **All platforms:** Short sentences. Concrete numbers. Operator ships — doesn't hype. Lead with proof. Single ask.

Weave in macro regime shifts, historical trade finance parallels (British Empire bills of exchange, correspondent banking empires), and micro trading angles (position sizing, timing windows, asymmetric setups) **when the audience thinks in those frames** — primarily VCs, OTC traders, and macro-oriented funds. Skip for freight forwarders and partnerships.

## Hard bans (delete and rewrite)

- "I'd love to connect"
- "I'm excited to share"
- "I hope this finds you well"
- "Just wanted to reach out"
- generic thesis praise without a specific tie-in
- "amazing", "transformative", "revolutionary", "game-changing"
- begging language ("would mean the world", "any time you can spare")
- soft closes ("when convenient", "no pressure") when a direct ask is cleaner
- sounding like a VC pitch deck

## Structure by message type

### LinkedIn Connection Request (under 280 chars)

Skeleton:
```
[Tie-in to them, one phrase] — building [BALBOA1 one-line]. [Specific reason for them]. Worth a 15-min look?
```

Example skeleton-filled:
```
Saw your Castle Island post on jurisdictional clarity — building BALBOA1, regulated PA stablecoin for maritime trade with 3K-vessel LOI committed. Curious if Tier 1 RWA + non-US issuance fits your map. Worth 15 mins?
```

Must hit 4 elements:
1. Tie-in (recent post / portfolio / thesis / mutual)
2. One-line identity (BALBOA1 = X for Y)
3. Why them specifically (not generic)
4. Explicit ask with time

### Follow-up 1 — Day 4 (under 60 words)

Add ONE new data point. Never just nudge.

Patterns:
- New proof point ("Just added [X] OTC partner / signed [Y] LOI / completed first $[Z] pilot transaction")
- Specific industry trigger ("Saw [GENIUS Act update / a comp raised at $80M post]")
- Question that invites a low-friction reply ("Curious if [X] is on your radar — happy to share what we're seeing")

Skeleton:
```
Quick bump, [Name].

[ONE new data point — concrete, dated, specific.]

Worth a quick look?
```

### Follow-up 2 — Day 10 (under 80 words)

Re-anchor on a different angle than the first touch. If FU1 was data-point, FU2 should be context. If you led with LOI initially, lead FU2 with regulatory or use-case angle.

## Opener archetypes (rotate across drafts in same day)

When drafting multiple Tier 0/1 messages, do not use the same opener twice. Rotate:

### Archetype 1: Mutual Momentum
Tie to something they posted/published/invested in within the last 90 days. Highest reply rate.

```
"Saw your [thread/podcast/investment] on [topic] — [your one-line + specific tie]."
```

### Archetype 2: Proof-First
Lead with the LOI / OTC / Canal flow numbers, then explain why.

```
"3,000-vessel LOI just signed for BALBOA1 maritime settlement. Panama IBC, regulated, OTC partners in place. [Why them]."
```

### Archetype 3: Thesis-Fit
Reference their portfolio company or stated thesis.

```
"Your [Portfolio Co] thesis rhymes with what we're building — [tie]. BALBOA1: Panama maritime stablecoin, first LOI for 3K vessels."
```

### Archetype 4: Mutual Connection (use sparingly, only when real)
```
"[Mutual] suggested I reach out. We're building BALBOA1 — [one line + tie to them]."
```

### Archetype 5: Macro Regime (for macro-oriented VCs, OTC traders, cycle thinkers)
Lead with the structural setup — dollar dominance erosion, trade fragmentation, stablecoin float as the new float business — then land BALBOA1 as the positioned play.

```
"Dollar weaponization + trade corridor fragmentation = the biggest asymmetric setup in settlement since Bretton Woods. BALBOA1 is the Panama play — regulated IBC, 3K-vessel LOI signed, OTC partners in place. [Why them]."
```

## Personalization sources — what counts

Strong (use these confidently):
- A specific post/thread/podcast they did in last 90 days
- A specific portfolio investment that rhymes with BALBOA1
- A published thesis or interview quote
- A mutual connection who's confirmed they'll vouch

Weak (use only if nothing else available):
- They work at firm X (everyone knows this)
- Their general space ("you focus on fintech")
- Generic shared interest

If you have only weak personalization, mark the draft `⚠️ NEEDS PERSONALIZATION` and Nick will manually upgrade before sending.

## Outreach silo nuances

### Venture & Investment
Cold emails work for partners with public emails. Otherwise LinkedIn connection request → intro after accept. Lead with LOI proof point + non-US regulatory angle. Reference their portfolio if there's a real rhyme. For macro-oriented VCs or funds that publish cycle/regime content, use Version D (macro regime lead) and Archetype 5 — frame BALBOA1 as the asymmetric positioned play, not just a product pitch.

### OTC & Market Makers
LinkedIn is fine but X DM often higher reply rate for traders. For X DMs: use full degen voice — raw, punchy, profanity where it lands. Lead with micro trading angle: flow profile, captive volume, position sizing opportunity, asymmetric timing window. They don't care about your story — they care about flow profile and counterparty risk. Reference their existing book. Macro regime framing works here if they post macro content.

### Partnerships (Canal Authority, Bladex, Inchcape)
LinkedIn only. Formal tone. Lead with regulatory framework + pilot interest. Avoid crypto-native language. Use "regulated digital settlement" not "stablecoin." Ask for an exploratory call, not a pitch.

### Freight Forwarders
LinkedIn. Pain-point lead (Version C). Specific dollar amounts ($200–400/wire, 3–5 day delays). Frame BALBOA1 as a tool that solves THEIR client's pain, with a referral upside.

### General Contacts (warm network)
Personal tone. Reference your shared context (where you met, what you worked on together). Lower-friction ask: "would love your eyes" rather than "would you invest."
